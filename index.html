Chocolate Cake

Prep Time: 45 mins | Serving: 4

🍫 Ingredients

  • 1 cup flour
  • 1/2 cup cocoa powder
  • 1/2 cup sugar
  • 1/2 tsp baking powder
  • 2 eggs

📝 Steps

  • Preheat the oven to 350°F.
  • Mix dry ingredients together.
  • Whisk in the eggs.
  • Pour into a baking dish.
  • Bake for 30 minutes.
🎉 Dish Completed! Enjoy your meal! 🎉
